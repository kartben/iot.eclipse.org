See you next year!

March 7, 2019

We regret to announce that the Eclipse Foundation has decided to cancel this years Open IoT Challenge 5.0. The main reason for this move is that unfortunately we did not receive a diverse enough set submissions to hold the meaningful competition we would all like to see.

We are looking forward to continuing this challenge next year with you and our sponsors: Intel, and CONTACT Software. We intend to bring forward a new and exciting format for this contest in 2020 and we are confident this will drive interest and participation. We also welcome your suggestions to improve the contest.

We would like to thank our sponsors and those who have submitted proposals to the IoT Challenge. We look forward to having your participation next year!


Thanks all for your understanding.

Our Sponsors

Our sponsors helped making this challenge possible by providing great prizes. If you want to sponsor the next Open IoT Challenge, please email us.
